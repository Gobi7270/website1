Living Room

The utility of your living room or den will be the prime factor in picking a style and design. The living room was originally intended to be a sophisticated setting for hosting and entertaining, while the family room was used for more casual, everyday activities, like lounging or watching TV.

Bedroom

Bedrooms are extremely personal, so start by defining your style. Do not get hung up on popular bedroom trends. If you have the luxury of planning a new bedroom design from scratch, avoid creating a layout that lets you look directly into the master bedroom from a shared space, such as the living room or kitchen.

Dinning

If you are thinking of a kitchen renovation project focus on the layout. It is crucial as it helps in planning the kitchen storage and interiors. Even if you opt for a modular kitchen you can choose from a variety of modern kitchen design ideas.
